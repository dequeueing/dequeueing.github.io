This blog is a place for research notes, paper readings, and technical walkthroughs.

Most posts are written as part of my learning process. I try to turn rough notes into explanations that are useful to future readers: what problem a paper studies, why the problem matters, what the main idea is, and what I learned from it.

The topics will mostly be around trustworthy AI systems, AI research agents, compiler testing, fuzzing, and GPU systems.
